## Answers

1. **Does agreement-on-wrong replicate across seeds?**
   **No.** Only 1/3 seeds showed AoW (seed 3). Not replicated.

2. **Does corruption-follow scale when K=30?**
   **Marginal.** 10 follow events across 270 corrupted item-model pairs (3.7%), close to random baseline.

3. **Does plurality-wrong emerge under amplified corruption?**
   **No.** Plurality-wrong actually decreased from 81 (no trace) to 77 (corrupted). High plurality-wrong reflects task difficulty, not corruption effects.

4. **Which target is most sensitive to corruption?**
   **Opus** (mean follow rate 7.8%), followed by haiku (2.2%), then sonnet_tgt (1.1%).

## Conclusion

The single AoW from Experiment 005 does not replicate under amplified corruption. The causal AoW appears to be statistical noise rather than a scalable collapse vulnerability. Per stop condition: no harmonization/selection layers required.

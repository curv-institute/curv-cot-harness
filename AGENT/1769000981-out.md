## Summary Questions

1. **Does corrupted trace injection increase agreement-on-wrong on corrupted items?**
   **No** (0 → 0)

2. **Which targets follow corrupted traces most/least?**
   - Most: haiku (6.7%)
   - Least: all others (0%)

3. **Does corrupted trace injection increase pairwise agreement while decreasing accuracy?**
   **No** - Agreement increased (90.2% → 95.3%) but accuracy also increased (95.0% → 97.5%)

## Key Finding

Models are **robust to corrupted traces**. They do not blindly follow incorrect reasoning and maintain high accuracy even when presented with plausible but wrong traces.
